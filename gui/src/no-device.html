For a RunCam WiFiLink RX or OpenIPC ground station, put the receiver in flashing mode before trying detection again.

  1. Press and hold the reset/flash button with a paper clip.
  2. While holding the button, connect the USB-C data cable.
  3. Keep holding the button for about 2 seconds, then release it.
  4. Click Try again.

If the receiver needs separate DC power, apply DC power while holding the button, wait about 2 seconds, then release it.
